# Mental Health

![Mental Health](/images/mental_health.jpg)

Mental health is an integral part of overall health and well-being. This section provides information on mental health promotion, common mental health conditions, and resources for support.

## Key Messages

- Mental health is just as important as physical health
- 1 in 4 people in the world experience psychological disorders
- Mental health conditions are treatable
- Seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness
- Early intervention leads to better outcomes
