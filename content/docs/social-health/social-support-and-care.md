# Social Support and Care

## Overview

Social support refers to the perception and actuality that one is cared for, has assistance available from other people, and that one is part of a supportive social network. These supportive resources can be emotional, informational, or practical.

Strong social support is associated with better physical and mental health outcomes. It can help buffer against stress and provide a sense of belonging and purpose.

---

## Types of Social Support

### Emotional Support
- Expressions of empathy, love, trust, and caring
- Someone to talk to about problems
- Feeling understood and valued

### Informational Support
- Advice and suggestions
- Information to help solve problems
- Guidance during difficult times

### Tangible/Instrumental Support
- Practical help and assistance
- Financial aid
- Help with daily tasks

### Companionship Support
- Sense of belonging
- Having people to spend time with
- Participating in shared activities

## Social Services Available

### In Turkey

Youth centers in provinces and districts, centers operating under municipalities and Social Solidarity Foundations offer social services.

**Available services include:**
- Economic support for families in need
- Psychological and social support
- Child and youth services
- Elderly care services
- Disability support services
- Women's support services

### Social and Economic Support Service

This service aims to identify families who cannot even achieve the lowest living standards and who cannot meet the basic needs of their children. Benefits include:
- Economic support until families achieve sufficient resources
- Psychological support
- Social support to mitigate health risks

## Building Social Support

### Tips for Strengthening Your Social Network

1. **Reach out to existing connections**: Don't wait for others to contact you
2. **Join groups or activities**: Find communities with shared interests
3. **Volunteer**: Helping others can build meaningful connections
4. **Be a good listener**: Strong relationships require give and take
5. **Stay connected with family**: Maintain regular contact
6. **Use technology wisely**: Social media can help maintain connections
7. **Seek professional help if needed**: Counselors can help with social skills

---

## FAQs

### Which social services can I access in Republic of Turkey?

Youth centers in provinces and districts, centers operating under municipalities and Social Solidarity Foundations offer social services. For assistance and services offered, contact:
- Alo 183 Social Support Line
- Ministry of Family and Social Services

### What is the purpose of Social and Economic Support Service?

This service aims to identify families who cannot even achieve the lowest living standards and who cannot meet the basic needs of their children and to offer them economic support until they can have sufficient economic sources. Besides, the families are offered psychological and social support to mitigate social and mental health risks.

### How can I build a stronger support network?

- Make an effort to stay in touch with friends and family
- Join clubs, groups, or communities with shared interests
- Volunteer in your community
- Be willing to both give and receive support
- Consider professional support groups if facing specific challenges
