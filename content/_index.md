# REACH Health Library

![REACH Logo](/images/ic_logo_with_text.png)

This documentation contains comprehensive health information extracted from the REACH mobile application data sources. The content covers health topics relevant to Turkey and Lebanon, organized into main categories with detailed subtopics.

- **[Healthy Lifestyle](/docs/healthy-lifestyle/)** - Information on maintaining a healthy lifestyle
- **[Physical Health](/docs/physical-health/)** - Medical information organized by specialty
- **[Mental Health](/docs/mental-health/)** - Mental health information and resources
- **[Social Health](/docs/social-health/)** - Social determinants of health and support systems
- **[Environmental Health](/docs/environmental-health/)** - Environmental factors affecting health
- **[COVID-19](/docs/covid-19/)** - Information about COVID-19 prevention and management
- **[Special Needs](/docs/special-needs/)** - Information for individuals with special needs
- **[Sexual and Reproductive Health](/docs/sexual-and-reproductive-health/)** - Comprehensive sexual and reproductive health information
- **[Self-Care](/docs/self-care/)** - Information on self-care practices and mental wellness
